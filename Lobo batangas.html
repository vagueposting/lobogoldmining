{ Lobo,Batangas }

The Philippines has 17 administrative regions. Region IV A, known as CALABARZON is composed of CAvite, LAguna, BAtangas, Rizal, and QueZON. It is in the southern Luzon; thus, it is also the second most densely populated region. CALABARZON also has contributed 16.8 percent in our GDP. Last 2016, the GDP increased by 4.8 percent. 5.5 percent of the region’s total output came from their agriculture, hunting, fishing and forestry. Our focus is Batangas, it is a province and a tourist destination known for its natural attractions such as Taal, Batangas is on the north of the province of Cavite, northeast and east of the province Laguna and Quezon respectively.
